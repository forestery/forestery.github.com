Russneft

Through its subsidiaries, Glencore has invested in the Russian upstream market with its partner OAO Russneft, by taking ownership in a diversified portfolio of oil producing assets. Russneft owns and operates a number of oil licences stretching from the Volga river in the west to the Siberian plains in the east and refineries in Krasnodar and Orsk. From February 2005 to date, through its subsidiaries Glencore has acquired 40-49% interests in various oil production subsidiaries of Russneft.

Production capacity

 

 

250,000 bbls crude oil per day (equivalent to 13.4 million tons per annum) on a 100% basis

Process

 

 

Production, development and exploration of numerous licensed oil fields
