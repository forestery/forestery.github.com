Sustainability reporting

GCP adds non-financial aspects to our internal corporate reporting, covering our performance on social, environmental, societal and compliance issues. Depending on the subject matter, routine reports may be made either annually or monthly. Major incidents, which we classify as “Class A,” must be reported within 24 hours.

Annual sustainability report

We publish annual public sustainability reports; the first of these is our report for 2010.

Our reporting follows the latest GRI guidelines, including the sector-specific supplement for metals and mining.

You can choose to access the first Glencore sustainability report as an interactive e-report for easy online reading, or download it as a pdf.
