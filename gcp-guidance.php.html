GCP guidance

We have created written guidance for our people to support the GCP principles, laying out the
basic requirements that are mandatory across Glencore.

This guidance is an integral part of GCP and has been rolled out to all our key staff. It provides background information on the programme and its objectives, as well as outlining GCP commitments and principles.

All our people are required to read the guidance document and implement it in their everyday work. This has generally included adapting individual operations' processes and compliance procedures. They are supported in this by their line managers and each operation's sustainability officers.

All our operations are also encouraged to offer feedback and suggestions for the continuous improvement of both the guidance and the GCP programme as a whole.
