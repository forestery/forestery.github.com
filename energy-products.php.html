Energy Products

Glencore's Energy Products business group is composed of two commodity departments:

  • Oil · Gas >
  • Coal · Coke >

Crude oil and oil products such as fuel oil, heating oil, gasoline, naphtha, jet fuel and liquefied petroleum gas, as well as steam coal and metallurgical coal and coke are physically marketed through these commodity departments.

To support the activities of these two departments, Glencore holds ownership interests, both directly and indirectly, in several strategically located coal mining operations as well as a diversified portfolio of oil producing assets.

Energy Products commodities are marketed and coordinated primarily by our offices in London, Baar, Stamford and Singapore.
