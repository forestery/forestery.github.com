Prodeco Group

Prodeco exports coal through its own port facility located in Santa Marta and other third party ports in Santa Marta. The coal is produced by its own Calenturitas and La Jagua mines as well as purchased from Colombian mines located in Cesar (La Jagua and La Loma). Prodeco opened its Calenturitas mine (located in La Loma) in July 2004. Glencore's initial interest was acquired through a subsidiary in 1995. The La Jagua mine is located in the Cesar province in Colombia. The Glencore interest was acquired through a subsidiary in January 2005.
