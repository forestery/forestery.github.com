Management

A central management team oversees high level decision making at Glencore, while also empowering our highly experienced department heads. Each of our department heads combines specific commodity experience with a proven ability to further develop Glencore’s global position. Our management remain keenly aware of both near and long-term considerations by attempting to address potential challenges before they even emerge.

As a major participant in the global flow of natural resources, we utilise our worldwide network of relationships and our own knowledge of industrial production to take advantage regional pricing differences where an imbalance of supply and demand may exist. Our commercial partners recognise the value of such experience and most of our relationships have developed over many years.
