Shanduka

Shanduka Coal, through its subsidiaries, owns 100% of the Graspan, Townlands, Bankfontein, Leeuwfontein and Lakeside mines, located near Middlelburg and Kendal in South Africa. Coal is sold both to the domestic market and exported through the Richards Bay Coal Terminal.  Glencore’s interests were acquired through subsidiaries between April 2006 and July 2007.
