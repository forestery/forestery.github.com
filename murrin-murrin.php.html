Murrin Murrin

Murrin Murrin is a nickel/cobalt mining and refining project in Western Australia. It is held through an unincorporated joint venture between Minara Resources Limited, MRE.AX (60%) and a subsidiary of Glencore. Glencore owns 96.9% of Minara Resources Limited. Glencore's initial interest in Murrin Murrin was acquired through a subsidiary in 1996.
