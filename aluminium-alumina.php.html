Alumina · Aluminium

Glencore and its subsidiaries are involved in the production of primary aluminium and are also large scale participants in the marketing of both aluminium and alumina sourced from world markets as well as from the Company’s owned industrial assets.

The supply sources and customer bases are highly diversified, and demonstrate our reputation for strong relationships in both markets. Glencore optimises the production systems of numerous integrated aluminium and alumina producers (including those owned via our subsidiaries) through the physical exchange of bauxite and alumina of varying origins. Our breadth of experience and integrated role in production enables us to provide market-leading expertise in the flow of these commodities.

Production assets

  • A subsidiary of Glencore owns 100% of the Sherwin Alumina refinery.
  • A subsidiary of Glencore has an economic interest of 44% (39% voting) in Century Aluminum Company, a NASDAQ-quoted company.
  • A subsidiary of Glencore owns 8.8% in United Company Rusal Limited (UCR), one of the world's largest aluminium producers with an annual production in 2010 of over 4 million metric tons of primary aluminium. In addition, UCR is the world's largest alumina producer with annual production in 2010 of over 7.8 million metric tons of alumina. The shareholding provides Glencore with a strategic and meaningful stake in one of the biggest and lowest cost aluminium producers in the world.
