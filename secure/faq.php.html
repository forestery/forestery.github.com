» When will dividends be paid?

Dividends are expected to be declared by the Board semi-annually (with the half year results and the preliminary full year results). They are paid twice yearly usually in September/October and May/June. A dividend is paid for each share, so the amount you receive depends on the number of shares you own. To receive the dividend, your name must be on the share register on the relevant dividend record date. If the shares are sold before the ex-dividend date or purchased after it, you will not be entitled to that dividend.

» How can I have my dividends paid?

Glencore shareholders on the Jersey main register are able to elect to receive their dividend payments in, Euros, Swiss Francs, US dollars and Pound Sterling. Pounds Sterling dividends can be paid by direct credit.

In order to receive your dividends directly into your bank account on dividend payment date, you will need to register your bank details on our Registrars' website.

» Who should I contact if I have lost my dividend cheque or have found an out-of-date dividend cheque?

If you have lost an historic dividend cheque you should contact our Registrar as soon as possible. They will arrange for the cheque to be stopped and then issue a replacement cheque.

» What about my Tax Voucher?

Provided you have registered your email address with our Registrar an electronic tax voucher will be sent to you by email at the time of each dividend payment. If you do not provide an email address, we will send you a paper consolidated tax voucher with your Final dividend payment. Your tax voucher will state details about both dividends paid during the tax year, how the amounts were calculated and the associated tax credit.

» Who should I contact if I have lost my dividend tax voucher?

If you require a replacement dividend tax voucher, please inform our Registrar who will arrange for a duplicate to be sent to you. There is a fee for this service.
