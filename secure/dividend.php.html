Dividend Information

The Company intends to pursue a progressive dividend policy with the intention of maintaining or increasing its total ordinary dividend each year.

Dividends are expected to be declared by the Board semi-annually (with the half-year results and the preliminary full year results). Interim dividends are expected to represent approximately one-third of the total dividend for any year. Dividends will be declared and paid in U.S. dollars, although Shareholders on the Jersey register will be able to elect to receive their dividend payments in Pounds Sterling, Euros or Swiss Francs. Shareholders on the Hong Kong branch register will receive their dividends in Hong Kong dollars.

The directors have declared a 2011 interim dividend of $ 0.05 per share amounting to $ 346 million. The dividend will be paid on 30 September 2011.

Dividend exchange rate for dividend payment of 30 September 2011

Glencore publishes its accounts in US dollars and the dividend payable on 30 September 2011 will be paid in US Dollars. Glencore has made arrangements for shareholders on the Jersey register who so elected by 12 September 2011 to receive this dividend in Pounds Sterling, Euros or Swiss Francs.

Shareholders on the Hong Kong (HK) branch register will receive their dividends in HK Dollars. The Pound Sterling, Euro, Swiss Franc or HK Dollar amount payable will be determined by reference to the closing mid exchange rates applicable to the US Dollar on Friday 23 September 2011, being 7 days prior to the dividend payment date.
